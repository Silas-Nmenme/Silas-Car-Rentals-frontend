Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

At Silas Car Rentals, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our car rental services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Driver's license information and identification documents
  • Payment information (credit card details, billing address)
  • Booking history and rental preferences

2.2 Usage Data

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device identifiers and location data
  • Website interaction patterns and preferences
  • Communication preferences and marketing opt-ins

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage car rental bookings
  • Verify identity and driving eligibility
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Payment processors for transaction processing
  • Insurance providers for coverage verification
  • Government authorities when legally required
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Third-party partners with your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure payment processing systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Data backup and recovery procedures

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Restrict certain data processing activities
  • Data portability (receive your data in a usable format)

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and fulfill bookings
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Maintain business records for accounting purposes

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and offers
  • Enable secure login and session management

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy
