Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Silas Car Rentals services, bookings, and policies.

How do I book a car?

Booking a car is simple:

  1. Browse our available cars on the website
  2. Select your preferred vehicle and rental dates
  3. Add to cart and proceed to checkout
  4. Sign up or log in to complete your booking
  5. Confirm your booking and make payment
What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need:

  • Valid driver's license (minimum 2 years)
  • Government-issued ID or passport
  • Credit card for payment and security deposit
  • Minimum age: 21 for standard vehicles, 25 for luxury cars
What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 24+ hours before pickup: Full refund
  • Less than 24 hours: 50% cancellation fee
  • No-show: Full rental charge applies
  • Early return: No refund for unused days
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo
  • Bank transfers (for corporate bookings)
  • Cash payments at our office
Is insurance included in the rental?

All rentals include basic third-party liability insurance. Additional coverage options are available:

  • Collision Damage Waiver (CDW)
  • Theft Protection (TP)
  • Personal Accident Insurance (PAI)
  • Super Collision Damage Waiver (SCDW)
Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, extensions are possible subject to availability. Contact us at least 2 hours before your scheduled return time. Additional charges may apply.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns are subject to charges:

  • Grace period: 29 minutes
  • After grace period: Full daily rate applies
  • Extensions must be requested in advance
Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery and pickup services within Lagos State. Additional charges apply based on distance.

What is your fuel policy?

Cars must be returned with the same fuel level as received. Refueling service is available at additional cost.

Can I drive outside Lagos?

Yes, you can drive within Nigeria. For international travel, special arrangements and additional insurance may be required.

What should I do in case of an accident?

In case of an accident:

  1. Ensure everyone's safety first
  2. Contact our 24/7 support line immediately
  3. Take photos of the damage
  4. File a police report if required
  5. Follow our accident procedures
Do you offer discounts?

We offer various discounts:

  • Long-term rental discounts
  • Corporate rates for businesses
  • Seasonal promotions
  • Loyalty program benefits
How do I report a problem with my rental?

Contact us immediately via phone, email, or our website. Our 24/7 support team will assist you promptly.
