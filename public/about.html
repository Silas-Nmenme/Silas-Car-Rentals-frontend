About Silas Car Rentals

Nigeria's trusted premium car rental service, delivering excellence in every journey since 2020.

Our Journey

2020 - The Beginning

Founded by Silas Nmenme with a vision to revolutionize car rental services in Nigeria. Started with just 5 premium vehicles and a commitment to excellence.

2021 - Rapid Growth

Expanded fleet to 50+ vehicles, serving over 1,000 satisfied customers. Introduced 24/7 customer support and mobile booking platform.

2022 - Market Leadership

Became Lagos's most trusted car rental platform with 150+ premium vehicles and 98% customer satisfaction rate.

2023 - Innovation

Launched advanced booking system, real-time vehicle tracking, and expanded services nationwide with 5,000+ happy customers.

2024 - Future Forward

Continuing to innovate with electric vehicle options, AI-powered recommendations, and expanded coverage across Nigeria.
